# Moment Generating Functions(MGF)

## Geometric MGF
- For $X$ ~ $Geom(p)$,
$$M(t) = E(e^Xt) = \frac{p}{1 - e^tq}$$
- for $qt$ < 1, i.e for t in $(-\infty, log(1/q))$ 

## Uniform MGF
- for $U$ ~ $Unif(a, b)$
$$M(t) = E(e^Ut) = \frac{e^tb - e^ta}{t(b-a)}$$
- for $t \neq 0$ and $M(0) = 1$
