# Logic

## Logical Connectives
- Suppose P and Q are statements
	- P $\wedge$ Q => Conjunction(*and*)
	- P $\vee$ Q => Disjunction(*or*)
	- ~ P => Not(*not*)

### Truth Table

| P   | Q   | P $\wedge$ Q | P $\vee$ Q | ~ P |
